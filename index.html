Keikyu Line / Tokyo Monorail


At the Information Counter, Transfer Tickets are distributed free of charge.
These tickets can be used to travel between Terminals 1 and 3 on the Keikyu Line / Tokyo Monorail.

➀ Please present your passport/flight tickets, etc. as confirmation at Information Counter on the basement 1st floor. You can get Transfer Tickets.
➁ Please present your Transfer Tickets to the station staff.

● You can use either the Keikyu Line or the Tokyo Monorail.
● Tickets are distributed only to customers transferring from domestic to international flight, international to domestic flights, and international to international flight.
